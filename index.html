About Me

My name is Ben Wright and I am a front-end web developer living in the Boston area. I have always enjoyed learning new things and using that knowledge to build cool things. That is why I got involved in the web. There is always something new to learn and something cool you can do with it.

I have experience building responsive web sites. These days people are accessing the web on a greater variety of devices and each has a different screen size. A web site may be viewed on anything from a large desktop monitor, mobile phone or a smart refrigerator. It is important to make the site look good no matter which device it is being viewed on.

In addition, I have experience working with JavaScript frameworks and libraries including jQuery, KnockoutJS and AngularJS. Using these powerful tools and several API's I have built several web apps.

Hobbies:

When I am not building websites or reading coding documentation, I am reading about other topics. Along the way I get an idea in my head that I want to try and it ends up being a new hobby. Here are a few examples of the things I have worked on.

  • Running: I have run 8 marathons and countless half marathons and smaller races.
  • Brewing beer: I have been brewing beer for over 9 years. I formulate my own recipes and build various contraptions to help brew the beer.
  • Coffee roasting: I roast my own coffee beans in a stovetop popcorn popper. I am working on automating the process with electronic motors.
  • Aquaponics: I have built a system to circulate the water from my fish tank through my indoor herb/vegetable garden. The plants and bacteria remove the fish waste to use as fertilizer while returning fresh clean water to the tank.
